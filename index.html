Motivation

What keeps you going? I have often wondered about this question. My answer: I would like to make my contribution to making our world a better place every day. There's so much to do out there. It's just overwhelming. And here I am. Backpacking on the web.

Backpacking on the web

It's nice to see how many people on the Internet share their knowledge, tackle projects together and do good things, such as developing open source software. I would like to build on this, learn and then also make a contribution to it. On my backpacking tour through the internet I have already got to know some technologies and tools that I could pack into my backpack. I am curious to see what else I will discover on my way.

Check out my first web projects

My first "web projects" feel like walking the first steps in the wilderness. Of course, these first steps are not yet at the level of a professional.
If you have time, take a look at my projects.
